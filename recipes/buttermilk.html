Buttermilk chicken recipe

This is a simple recipe for a quick and delicious buttermilk chicken to cook up for dinner

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • seasoning
  • buttermilk
  • flour
  • peanut oil

Steps:

  1. Toss the chicken in seasoning, marinate in buttermilk overnight
  2. Make the seasoned flour
  3. Dredge each buttermilk-soaked chicken piece in flour and transfer to a plate
  4. Fry the chicken until 165 degrees Farenheit
  5. Drain and cool over a cooling rack or a paper towel
