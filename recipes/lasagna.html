Lasagna recipe

Quick and simple recipe for the well-known italian dish

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and season
  3. Assemble the lasagana according to the detailed recipe
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 mins
