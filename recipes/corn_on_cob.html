Corn on cob recipe

Delicious and simple corn on the cob recipe to make your kids happy

Ingredients:

  • 2tbl spoons white sugar
  • 1tbl spoon lemon juice
  • 6 ears corn on cob, husked

Steps:

  1. Fill a large pot about 3/4 full of water, bring to a boil, add sugar and lemon juice
  2. Gently place ears into boiling water, cover, turn of the heat, cook until tender
